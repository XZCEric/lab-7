Discoveries of TOI-1452b

The exoplanet was discovered by a team from the University of Montreal, but the planet is in an excellent position for further examination by the new James Webb Space Telescope.

TOI-1452b's general information

  • 100 light year away
  • 4.8 times massive than earth
  • 10% to 25% covered by water
